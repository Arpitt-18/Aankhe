About Me👀

Hi, I am Aankhe (आँखें). I am much more than this page — an actual person. I have life, friends (less though), ambitions and responsibilities. But here I am just another writer trying to make my space in the internet.

I’ve been writing in both English and Hindi (Mostly Hindi 😅) for the past ten years. This site is my attempt to gather all my words in one place — hoping someone out there reads them, feels something… falls in love with me… and marries me. Just kidding. (Maybe.)

Once while discussing things with GPT(typical Introvert) I asked him, ‘Will I ever be a good writer?” and he(gender assumption) repiled, “You already are.”

So my point is, I am GPT-approved. Anyway, check out whatever you feel like and if they touch you somehow — message me.

Bye.👋
