I want to write something

I want to write something
Something that not pushes people
Into a realm of unknown
But lets them choose
A world of there own

Flowers? No? fine.
Let us bloom misery
Let skies be red
Let nature be sad
Let us drink tears
And eat our destiny

Let's see beyond any truth
Even if it's a lie
Because it's beautiful
It's you.
